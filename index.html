Pillars Of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Innovation is the process of creating new ideas, products, or methods to drive progress and solve problems.

Sustainability

Sustainability ensures meeting present needs while preserving resources and the environment for the future.

Infrastructure

Infrastructure comprises essential facilities like roads, power, and systems supporting economic growth.

Empowerment

Empowerment enables individuals or groups to gain confidence, skills, and control over their lives.

Digital Transformation

Digital Transformation integrates technology to enhance operations, customer value, and competitiveness.

Green Energy

Green energy uses renewable resources like solar, wind, and hydropower for sustainable, eco-friendly power.

Modern Infrastructure

Modern infrastructure integrates technology, sustainability, and efficiency to support urban and economic growth.
